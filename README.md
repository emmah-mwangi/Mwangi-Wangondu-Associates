## About Us

Founded in 2010, **Mwangi Wangondu & Associates** is a premier audit and advisory firm serving clients across Kenya and East Africa. We are a registered firm of Certified Public Accountants offering comprehensive audit, assurance, and financial capacity development services with over 15 years of operational excellence.

### Mission
To empower businesses and organizations with reliable, transparent, and strategic financial solutions that foster accountability, growth, and long-term sustainability.

### Vision
To be the leading audit and advisory partner in Africa — transforming the way financial systems support innovation, development, and ethical leadership across sectors.
